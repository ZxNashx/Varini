The Suevi Federation and the Varini Tribe

The Suevi federation was a large group of tribes who had migrated from southern Sweden to northern Germany. They were distinguished from other Germanic tribes because they would twist their hair and bind it in a knot. (Tacitus Germania, p.31) The Suevi were the only group from the pool of Germanic tribes that did this. They were also a group of tribes that were defended by their surroundings and took part in the worship of Herthus. (Tacitus, Germania, p.32)

The Varini were a tribe that were part of the Suevi.
